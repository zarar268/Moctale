Overview

Plastic Memories is a heartfelt sci-fi romance anime set in a future where human-like androids, called Giftias, possess limited lifespans. The story follows Tsukasa Mizugaki, a young employee at the SAI Corporation, who works in the Terminal Service Department — responsible for retrieving Giftias nearing the end of their lives. There, he partners with Isla, a kind and fragile Giftia with only a short time left. As they collect memories and face the inevitable, the series explores love, loss, and what it means to cherish every moment. Beautifully emotional and bittersweet, Plastic Memories leaves a lasting impact on anyone who watches it.
