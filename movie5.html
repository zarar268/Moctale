Overview

“Evil Dead Rise” is an American horror film directed by Lee Cronin.
The story follows two sisters, Ellie and Beth, who reunite in a Los Angeles apartment building and encounter the unleashing of the Necronomicon’s demonic evil.
As the demonic forces wreak havoc, the sisters fight for survival in intense, terrifying sequences.
Known for its brutal horror, creative kills, and dark atmosphere, “Evil Dead Rise” expands the franchise’s legacy in a modern urban setting.
