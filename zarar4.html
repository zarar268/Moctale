Overview

“Vinland Saga” is a Japanese historical action-adventure anime based on the manga by Makoto Yukimura.
Set in the Viking era, it follows Thorfinn, a young warrior seeking revenge against Askeladd — the man who killed his father. The series blends intense battles, rich character arcs, and themes of war, revenge, and redemption as Thorfinn journeys from violence toward discovering a new path.
