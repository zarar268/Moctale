Overview

Brooklyn Nine-Nine is a sharp, hilarious police sitcom created by Dan Goor and Michael Schur. Set in Brooklyn’s 99th precinct, the show follows the antics of the talented yet immature detective Jake Peralta and his team as they solve crimes while navigating friendships and bureaucracy. With witty humor, heartfelt moments, and a standout cast including Andy Samberg, Terry Crews, and Andre Braugher, Brooklyn Nine-Nine stands out as one of the most beloved comedies of the decade — balancing laughter, social awareness, and pure fun. No doubt, it’s “cool, cool, cool, cool, cool.”
