Overview

Tokyo Ghoul is a Japanese anime based on the manga by Sui Ishida. It follows Ken Kaneki, a shy college student who becomes half-ghoul after a near-fatal encounter with one. Trapped between the human and ghoul worlds, Kaneki struggles with his identity and survival as he learns to navigate a violent and tragic society. The series explores deep psychological conflict, dark themes, and the meaning of humanity.
