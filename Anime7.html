Overview

One Punch Man is a Japanese anime based on the webcomic by ONE and the manga illustrated by Yusuke Murata. The story follows Saitama, an ordinary man who trains so hard that he becomes the world’s strongest hero, defeating any opponent with a single punch. While his power is unmatched, Saitama struggles with boredom and the lack of a real challenge. Alongside his disciple Genos, he navigates the Hero Association, fighting powerful monsters and villains while searching for meaning in battle. The anime is praised for its mix of epic action and parody of superhero tropes.
