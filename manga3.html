Overview

“My Hero Academia” by Kōhei Horikoshi follows Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without superpowers in a world where almost everyone has “Quirks.”
After a chance encounter with his idol All Might, he inherits the legendary Quirk “One For All.”
Enrolling at U.A. High School, he trains to become the world’s greatest hero.
The manga mixes high-energy battles with themes of friendship, sacrifice, and heroism.
