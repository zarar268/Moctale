Overview

Blue Lock is a Japanese sports anime based on the manga written by Muneyuki Kaneshiro and illustrated by Yusuke Nomura.
The story begins after Japan’s devastating loss at the 2018 FIFA World Cup. To build the world's strongest egoist striker, the Japanese Football Union initiates the Blue Lock project — a brutal and competitive training program.
The series focuses on Yoichi Isagi, a talented but self-doubting forward, who enters Blue Lock to pursue his dream of becoming Japan’s ace striker. It’s a fierce journey of self-discovery, rivalry, and survival.
