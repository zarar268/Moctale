Overview

Death Note is a Japanese anime adapted from the manga by Tsugumi Ohba and Takeshi Obata. The story follows Light Yagami, a brilliant student who discovers a supernatural notebook dropped by the Shinigami, Ryuk. Any name written in the book dies instantly, giving Light the power to reshape the world by eliminating criminals. However, his plans spark a thrilling battle of wits against the mysterious detective known only as L. The anime is praised for its psychological tension, moral dilemmas, and mind games between genius rivals.
