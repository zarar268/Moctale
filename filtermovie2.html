Overview

“Mad Max: Fury Road” is a post-apocalyptic action film directed by George Miller, serving as both a sequel and reboot of the Mad Max franchise.
The story follows Max Rockatansky (Tom Hardy), a lone drifter captured by warlord Immortan Joe, and Imperator Furiosa (Charlize Theron), who rebels against tyranny.
As they race across the desert in a high-octane chase, the film explores survival, redemption, and the fight for freedom in a desolate wasteland.
Praised for its intense action sequences, cinematography, and practical effects, it is regarded as one of the best action films ever made.
