Overview

Parks and Recreation is a beloved American mockumentary sitcom created by Greg Daniels and Michael Schur. Set in the fictional town of Pawnee, Indiana, the show follows Leslie Knope, a passionate and optimistic government employee who dreams of improving her community. With heartfelt humor, quirky characters, and sharp satire of small-town politics, Parks and Recreation delivers both laughs and inspiration. Its unique blend of comedy and sincerity, along with standout performances by Amy Poehler and Nick Offerman, have made it a modern classic.
