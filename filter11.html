Overview

Serial Experiments Lain is a groundbreaking psychological and cyberpunk anime that dives deep into themes of identity, technology, and existence. The story follows Lain Iwakura, a quiet teenage girl who becomes entangled in “The Wired,” a mysterious digital network that blurs the line between reality and the virtual world. As she uncovers unsettling truths about herself and society, the show explores consciousness, isolation, and the evolving nature of human connection in the digital age. Serial Experiments Lain is a haunting and thought-provoking masterpiece that continues to influence modern sci-fi storytelling.
