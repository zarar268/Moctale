Overview

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is an action-adventure game developed by FromSoftware. Players control a shinobi named Wolf in Sengoku-era Japan, tasked with protecting a young lord and seeking revenge against those who wronged him. The game is renowned for its challenging combat, precise parrying mechanics, and deep stealth and exploration elements.
