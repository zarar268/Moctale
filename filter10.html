Overview

Paranoia Agent is a psychological thriller by Satoshi Kon that explores fear, anxiety, and collective delusion in modern society. The story follows a series of mysterious attacks by a boy on rollerblades known as “Lil’ Slugger”. As detectives investigate, paranoia spreads among citizens — revealing how their mental struggles and societal pressures intertwine. Deeply symbolic and unsettling, Paranoia Agent is a mind-bending exploration of human psyche, identity, and mass hysteria.
