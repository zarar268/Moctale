Overview

Boogiepop Phantom is a haunting psychological and supernatural anime set in a city plagued by strange occurrences after a mysterious pillar of light appears in the sky. The story unfolds through multiple interwoven perspectives, exploring fear, identity, trauma, and the hidden darkness within ordinary lives. At the center of it all is Boogiepop — an enigmatic entity said to appear when chaos threatens the world. With its nonlinear storytelling, eerie visuals, and chilling atmosphere, Boogiepop Phantom challenges the viewer’s perception of reality and the human psyche.
