Overview

Tokyo Revengers is a Japanese anime adaptation of Ken Wakui’s popular manga. The series follows Takemichi Hanagaki, a young man who discovers he has the ability to travel back in time. Determined to save his ex-girlfriend Hinata Tachibana from being killed by the Tokyo Manji Gang, he vows to rewrite the past. Along the way, Takemichi faces violent gang wars, builds bonds with powerful delinquents, and learns the true meaning of courage and sacrifice. The anime is a gripping mix of action, time travel, and emotional drama.
