Overview

“Money Heist” (La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish crime-drama series about a criminal mastermind known as The Professor, who recruits eight skilled thieves to carry out ambitious heists — first on the Royal Mint of Spain, and later on the Bank of Spain. The show combines high-stakes action, emotional twists, and deep character arcs with social commentary.
