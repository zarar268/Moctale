Overview

“Lookism” by Park Tae-jun tells the story of Park Hyung-seok, a teenager who is bullied for being overweight and unattractive.
One day, he mysteriously gains a second body — handsome, tall, and strong.
Switching between his two forms, he navigates high school, friendships, and fights, while exploring society’s obsession with looks.
The series balances comedy, action, and serious commentary on discrimination and self-acceptance.
