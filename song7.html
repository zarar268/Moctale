Chaleya

“Chaleya” is a romantic track from Shah Rukh Khan’s blockbuster film Jawan (2023). Sung by Arijit Singh and Shilpa Rao, with music composed by Anirudh Ravichander, the song beautifully blends heartfelt vocals with a modern melodic arrangement. Its soothing lyrics and emotional depth make it one of the most loved love songs of recent years.
