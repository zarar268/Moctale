Overview

Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day is a deeply emotional coming-of-age anime that explores grief, friendship, and the pain of moving on. The story follows Jinta Yadomi, a withdrawn boy who starts seeing the ghost of his childhood friend Menma, who died years ago. Menma’s appearance reunites their estranged group of friends as they try to fulfill her last wish and come to terms with their shared past. With heartfelt storytelling and powerful themes of loss and healing, Anohana is widely praised as one of the most touching anime ever made.
