Overview

Resident Evil 4 is a modern remake of the 2005 classic, developed by Capcom. Players assume the role of Leon S. Kennedy, a U.S. agent on a mission to rescue the President’s daughter from a mysterious cult in a remote European village. Enhanced with updated graphics, refined gameplay mechanics, and deeper storytelling, the remake successfully balances nostalgia with fresh horror-action experiences for new and returning players alike.
