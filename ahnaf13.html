Overview

Dragon Ball Z is a legendary anime series that continues the adventures of Goku as he defends Earth and the universe from increasingly powerful foes.
Packed with intense battles, iconic transformations like Super Saiyan, and themes of friendship, sacrifice, and perseverance, DBZ shaped generations of anime fans worldwide.
With arcs like Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu, Dragon Ball Z remains a cornerstone of shonen anime and pop culture history.
