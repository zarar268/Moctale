Overview

“Bone” is a dark fantasy manhwa written by **Juder**. The story begins when a mysterious man awakens, his entire body made of bone, with no memories of who he is or how he came to be. Thrown into a brutal, violent world, he must fight for survival, uncover hidden truths, and learn the horrifying secrets of his existence. The series mixes psychological tension, supernatural combat, and deep mystery.
