Overview

“My Dress-Up Darling” (Sono Bisque Doll wa Koi wo Suru) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Shinichi Fukuda.
The story follows Wakana Gojo, a high school boy who loves crafting hina dolls, and Marin Kitagawa, a popular girl who’s into cosplay.
They team up as Marin asks Gojo to help make her costumes, sparking a heart-warming mix of friendship, romance, and self-expression.
Known for its charming characters and detailed cosplay designs, the series blends comedy, slice-of-life and romance themes beautifully.
