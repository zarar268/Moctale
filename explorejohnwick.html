Overview

*John Wick* follows Keanu Reeves as a legendary hitman forced out of retirement after gangsters kill his dog — a final gift from his late wife. Known for its incredible fight choreography, stylish gun-fu combat, and emotional storytelling, John Wick has become one of the most iconic action franchises in cinema.
