Overview

Spider-Man: Miles Morales is an action-adventure game developed by Insomniac Games. Players control Miles Morales as the new Spider-Man, learning to harness his unique powers while protecting New York City from a high-tech criminal organization. The game features dynamic combat, web-swinging traversal, and a rich narrative exploring identity, responsibility, and heroism.
