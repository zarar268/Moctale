Overview

Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy anime adapted from Gege Akutami’s hit manga. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a kind-hearted teenager who becomes involved in the world of Jujutsu Sorcerers after swallowing a cursed object — the finger of the legendary demon Sukuna. Enrolled at the Tokyo Jujutsu High under the mentorship of Satoru Gojo, Yuji trains to fight curses and protect humanity. With stunning fights, deep emotional arcs, and supernatural lore, Jujutsu Kaisen has become one of the most popular modern shonen anime worldwide.
