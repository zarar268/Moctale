Overview

The Last of Us is a critically acclaimed action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog. Set in a post-apocalyptic United States, players follow Joel and a young girl named Ellie as they navigate a dangerous world ravaged by a deadly fungal infection. With its emotionally driven narrative, brutal combat, and immersive environments, the game redefined storytelling in video games and remains one of the most impactful titles of its generation.
