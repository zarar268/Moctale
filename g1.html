Overview

Resident Evil Village is a survival horror game developed by Capcom and the eighth main entry in the Resident Evil series. Set in a mysterious European village, the game follows Ethan Winters as he searches for his kidnapped daughter, uncovering terrifying secrets along the way. With intense first-person action, eerie environments, and iconic villains like Lady Dimitrescu, the game masterfully blends psychological horror and gripping combat.
