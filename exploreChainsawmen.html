Overview

Chainsaw Man – The Movie: Reze Arc is an anime film sequel to the first season of *Chainsaw Man*. After the events of season one, Denji now works as a Devil Hunter in Public Safety Division 4. After going on a date with Makima, he meets Reze, a mysterious girl working at a café. Their meeting sets in motion dramatic conflicts, emotional tension, and intense battles. The film adapts the “Bomb Girl” arc from the manga (chapters 40–52).
