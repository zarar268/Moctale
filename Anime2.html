Overview

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese anime adapted from Koyoharu Gotouge’s manga. It follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kindhearted boy who becomes a Demon Slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons and his sister Nezuko is turned into one. The series blends intense sword-fighting action with emotional storytelling, focusing on Tanjiro’s journey to find a cure for Nezuko and avenge his family.
