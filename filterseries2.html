Overview

The Punisher is a gritty, action-packed series created by Steve Lightfoot and based on the Marvel Comics character. It follows Frank Castle, a former Marine who becomes the vigilante known as The Punisher after his family is brutally murdered. Seeking vengeance against those responsible and justice for the powerless, Frank wages a one-man war against crime, corruption, and conspiracy. The Punisher combines intense combat, psychological depth, and raw emotion, making it one of Marvel’s darkest and most grounded shows.
