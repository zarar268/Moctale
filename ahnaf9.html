Overview

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan.
The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during dreams. Offered a chance to have his criminal record erased, he must pull off the impossible: inception — planting an idea into someone’s mind.
With intense visuals, layered realities, and a haunting score, Inception is a cinematic experience that challenges perception and reality at every turn.
