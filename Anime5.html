Overview

Bleach is a Japanese anime adapted from Tite Kubo’s best-selling manga. It follows Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who accidentally obtains the powers of a Soul Reaper. Tasked with defending humans from evil spirits known as Hollows, he also guides departed souls to the afterlife. As the story unfolds, Ichigo faces powerful foes, discovers his true origins, and fights to protect both the human world and Soul Society. The anime is known for its intense battles, iconic Bankai transformations, and deep lore.
