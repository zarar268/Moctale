Overview

“Dan Da Dan” is a Japanese manga series created by Yukinobu Tatsu.
The story follows Momo Ayase and Ken Takakura who encounter strange supernatural beings, UFOs, and curses.
Mixing comedy, romance, action, and sci-fi themes, the series stands out for its fast-paced, quirky storytelling and vibrant art style.
Known for its fresh take on supernatural and sci-fi elements, it has become one of the standout new generation manga.
