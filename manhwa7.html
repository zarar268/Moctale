Overview

“The Beginning After The End” is a Korean webtoon written by King Grey and illustrated by Fuyuki23.
The story follows King Grey, a once-powerful monarch reincarnated into a new world with magical powers.
As he navigates his second life, he faces challenges, builds relationships, and grows stronger while protecting those he loves.
Known for its immersive world-building, exciting battles, and deep character development, this webtoon is a favorite among fantasy and action fans.
