Overview

Your Lie in April (Shigatsu wa Kimi no Uso) is a Japanese anime based on Naoshi Arakawa’s manga. It tells the story of Kousei Arima, a piano prodigy who loses the ability to hear the sound of his own playing after his mother’s death. His world changes when he meets Kaori Miyazono, a free-spirited violinist who helps him rediscover music, passion, and life itself. Filled with emotion and beautiful performances, the series explores love, loss, and the healing power of art.
