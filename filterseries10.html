Overview

Fleabag is a dark British comedy-drama created and written by Phoebe Waller-Bridge, based on her one-woman stage play. The show follows a witty yet emotionally fractured woman navigating grief, guilt, love, and family dysfunction while breaking the fourth wall to share her unfiltered thoughts with the audience. Set in London, Fleabag delivers sharp humor and deep introspection, blending comedy and tragedy in a uniquely personal way. The show won multiple Emmy Awards including Best Comedy Series and Best Actress for Waller-Bridge.
