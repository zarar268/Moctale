Overview

“Nobody” is an American action-thriller film directed by Ilya Naishuller.
The story follows Hutch Mansell, an underestimated suburban dad, whose past violent skills resurface after his family is threatened.
As Hutch takes on dangerous criminals, the film explores revenge, personal awakening, and hidden talents.
Known for its intense fight sequences, dark humor, and fast-paced action, “Nobody” has been praised for revitalizing the action genre.
