Overview

“Berserk” is a Japanese dark fantasy manga series written and illustrated by Kentaro Miura.
The story follows Guts, a lone mercenary with a tragic past, and Griffith, the charismatic leader of the Band of the Hawk.
Known for its intense action, deep psychological themes and grim atmosphere, Berserk explores fate, revenge, sacrifice and the struggle against darkness.
With its stunning art and mature storytelling, Berserk is regarded as one of the most influential dark fantasy mangas of all time.
