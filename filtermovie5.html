Overview

“Pride & Prejudice” is a romantic drama film directed by Joe Wright, based on Jane Austen’s beloved novel.
Starring Keira Knightley as Elizabeth Bennet and Matthew Macfadyen as Mr. Darcy, the story explores class, pride, and the misunderstandings that stand in the way of love.
Set in early 19th-century England, it captures the tension between personal freedom and social expectation.
With exquisite cinematography, a stirring score by Dario Marianelli, and acclaimed performances, it remains one of the most graceful literary adaptations ever made.
