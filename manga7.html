Overview

“Mob Psycho 100” is a Japanese manga series created by ONE.
It follows Shigeo “Mob” Kageyama, a seemingly ordinary middle-school boy who actually possesses immense psychic powers.
While trying to live a normal life, Mob struggles to control his emotions which directly influence his abilities, leading to explosive consequences.
Mixing humor, action, and heartfelt themes, the series is praised for its unique art style and emotional depth.
