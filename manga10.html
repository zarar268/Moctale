Overview

“Vagabond” is a Japanese historical samurai manga series written and illustrated by Takehiko Inoue.
The story is a fictionalized account of the life of Miyamoto Musashi, one of Japan’s most famous swordsmen.
It follows Musashi’s journey from a violent youth to a master swordsman seeking enlightenment and self-discovery.
Known for its realistic art, intense duels and philosophical themes, Vagabond is widely acclaimed as one of the greatest samurai mangas ever created.
