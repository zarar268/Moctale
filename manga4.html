Overview

“Uzumaki” by Junji Ito is a psychological horror manga set in the small Japanese town of Kurouzu-cho.
The residents of the town gradually fall under the curse of spirals (uzumaki).
The story follows Kirie Goshima and her boyfriend Shuichi Saito as they try to understand and survive the bizarre curse.
This manga is famous for its atmospheric, disturbing, and haunting visuals.
