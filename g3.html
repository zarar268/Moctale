Overview

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Games. Set in the late 1800s, players take the role of Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang, navigating life in the fading Wild West. The game features a vast, living world with a gripping story, dynamic weather, hunting, exploration, and an online multiplayer mode, delivering one of the most immersive gaming experiences ever.
