Overview

“The Shawshank Redemption” is an American drama film directed by Frank Darabont, based on a novella by Stephen King.
The story follows Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murder, as he navigates life in Shawshank State Penitentiary.
Through friendship with Red and personal resilience, Andy inspires hope and fights for freedom in the face of despair.
Known for its powerful storytelling, deep character development, and emotional impact, it is widely regarded as one of the greatest films of all time.
