Suzume Song

“Suzume Song” is the beautiful theme track from Makoto Shinkai’s acclaimed anime film Suzume (2022). Performed by RADWIMPS in collaboration with Toaka, this soundtrack blends emotional vocals with a mesmerizing melodic arrangement. Its heartfelt composition perfectly captures the spirit of adventure, wonder and emotion in the movie.
