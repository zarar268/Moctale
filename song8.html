O Rangrez

“O Rangrez” from the movie Bhaag Milkha Bhaag (2013) is a soulful romantic track composed by Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy. Sung by Javed Bashir and Shreya Ghoshal, the song beautifully blends Indian classical melodies with modern orchestration. It captures the essence of deep love, devotion, and longing, becoming one of the most celebrated songs of the film.
