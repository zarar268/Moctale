Overview

“Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind” is a genre-bending romantic sci-fi drama directed by Michel Gondry and written by Charlie Kaufman.
Starring Jim Carrey and Kate Winslet, the film explores the nature of memory, love, and pain through the story of a couple who undergo a procedure to erase each other from their minds.
As memories fade, the film journeys through a surreal dreamscape of heartbreak, nostalgia, and rediscovery.
It’s a poetic, visually inventive, and emotionally resonant masterpiece about how even the most painful relationships leave traces worth remembering.
