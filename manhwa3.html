Overview

“Study Group” is a Korean webtoon written by Han Soo and illustrated by Jin Ah.
The story follows a group of high school students who form a study club to help each other improve academically.
Amidst their academic struggles, they navigate friendships, romantic feelings, and personal growth.
Known for its heartwarming comedy, relatable characters, and slice-of-life storytelling, Study Group resonates with readers of all ages.
