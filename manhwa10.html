Overview

Solo Leveling follows Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter of all mankind, in a world where portals connect Earth to dangerous dungeons. After a near-death experience, he gains a mysterious power that allows him to level up without limit. With this new ability, he rises from the weakest hunter to humanity’s greatest weapon, facing gods, monarchs, and the truth behind his power.
