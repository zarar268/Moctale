Overview

Black Clover is a Japanese anime based on Yūki Tabata’s manga. It follows Asta and Yuno, two orphans raised together who dream of becoming the Wizard King. In a world where magic is everything, Asta is born without magic but trains his body to overcome this. The series features intense magical battles, rivalries, and a strong theme of perseverance.
