Overview

“Lost in Translation” is a poignant romantic drama written and directed by Sofia Coppola, starring Bill Murray and Scarlett Johansson.
Set in Tokyo, it tells the story of two lonely strangers who form a deep, fleeting connection while navigating isolation and cultural dislocation.
Through subtle humor, tender silences, and emotional honesty, it explores themes of alienation, midlife crisis, and unspoken intimacy.
With gorgeous cinematography, a haunting soundtrack, and understated performances, it’s a timeless meditation on what it means to be seen and understood.
