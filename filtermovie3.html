Overview

“Gladiator” is an epic historical drama film directed by Ridley Scott and starring Russell Crowe as Maximus Decimus Meridius.
Set in ancient Rome, it follows a betrayed general who seeks vengeance against the corrupt emperor Commodus who murdered his family and sent him into slavery.
Rising as a gladiator, Maximus inspires hope and defiance in the arena as he fights for honor, freedom, and justice.
The film is acclaimed for its direction, performances, music by Hans Zimmer, and grand storytelling—earning multiple Academy Awards including Best Picture.
