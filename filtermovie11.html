Overview

“Her” is a futuristic romantic drama directed by Spike Jonze, starring Joaquin Phoenix as Theodore Twombly and Scarlett Johansson as the voice of Samantha.
Set in near-future Los Angeles, it tells the story of a lonely writer who develops an intimate relationship with an advanced AI operating system.
Blending science fiction and emotional realism, it explores love, loneliness, and what it means to connect in a digital age.
With evocative visuals, haunting music by Arcade Fire, and heartfelt performances, it remains one of the most original love stories of the 21st century.
