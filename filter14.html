Overview

Laid-Back Camp (Yuru Camp△) is a serene slice-of-life anime about friendship, nature, and the quiet joy of camping. It follows Rin Shima, a solo camper who loves peaceful trips in the Japanese wilderness, and Nadeshiko Kagamihara, an energetic newcomer who falls in love with camping after meeting Rin. Together with the Outdoor Activities Club, they explore the beauty of simple living, campfire cooking, and warm companionship. The series is celebrated for its calming atmosphere, cozy art style, and gentle humor.
