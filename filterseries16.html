Overview

Friends is a timeless sitcom that follows the lives, relationships, and hilarious misadventures of six close-knit friends living in New York City — Rachel, Ross, Monica, Chandler, Joey, and Phoebe. Created by David Crane and Marta Kauffman, the show became a global phenomenon known for its sharp wit, iconic moments, and unforgettable catchphrases. Whether it’s coffee at Central Perk or the ups and downs of love and friendship, Friends remains one of the most beloved comedies of all time. “I’ll be there for you,” indeed.
