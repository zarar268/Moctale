Overview

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Games. Set in the sprawling city of Los Santos, players can switch between three main characters—Michael, Franklin, and Trevor—to explore, complete missions, or engage in various activities. The game features a dynamic world with a deep storyline, online multiplayer mode, and endless opportunities for chaos and exploration.
