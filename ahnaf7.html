Overview

Lookism is a Korean animated series adapted from the popular webtoon of the same name by Park Tae-jun.
The story follows Park Hyung Suk, an overweight and bullied high school student who mysteriously wakes up in a tall, handsome body. By switching between his two bodies, he experiences society's harsh beauty standards from two very different perspectives.
With themes of identity, bullying, self-worth, and redemption, Lookism blends social commentary with action and emotional drama.
